Nicholas Young

producer / broadcaster / photographer / filmmaker

Iconic photos that capture emotion. Motion pictures that hold your attention with a powerful story. Broadcast programs that remind you of radio's golden age. These scenarios are where Nicholas is most at home.

Nicholas' voice has been heard by millions around the globe thanks to his adoption of podcasting in early 2005, not to mention his appearances on terrestrial radio. And as a photographer, director, and producer, he's had the chance to work with some of best names in independent music to create striking images, artistic short-films, music videos, and impactful press materials.

His latest venture, Original Machine, is a modern-day artist services firm - offering a unique approach to design, management, and distribution.
