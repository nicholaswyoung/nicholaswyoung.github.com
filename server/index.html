Nicholas Young

speaker / broadcaster / photographer / filmmaker

Iconic photos that capture emotion. Motion pictures that hold your attention with a powerful story. Broadcast programs that remind you of radio's golden age. These scenarios are where Nicholas is most at home.

Nicholas' voice has been heard by millions around the globe thanks to his early adoption of podcasting in early 2005, not to mention his numerous appearances on terrestrial radio. And as a photographer, director, and producer, he's had the chance to work with some of best names in independent music - to create striking images, meaningful short-films, documentaries, and impactful press materials.

After a seven-year sojourn in the world of professional software development, it's time to bring back the old hustle to new markets. With the re-launch of his creative-intelligence firm Original Machine, and the small but mighty Machine.FM broadcasting network, his voice - and the voice of friends - is louder than ever.
